Meet Your Speech-Language Pathologist

Wendy Arlint, M.S., CCC-SLP

Speech-Language Pathologist

Wendy brings extensive experience in helping individuals of all ages overcome communication challenges. She is dedicated to providing evidence-based treatments in a supportive, personalized environment.

Whether your child is working on clear speech, getting ready for school, or learning to use AAC to communicate, Wendy is here to support your family every step of the way. Her approach is play-based, supportive, and focused on helping children communicate confidently in their daily lives.

Professional Credentials

  • Master of Science in Speech-Language Pathology
  • ASHA Certified (Certificate of Clinical Competence in SLP)
  • Licensed Speech-Language Pathologist
  • Ongoing continuing education in latest treatment methods

Areas of Specialization

  • Evaluation and treatment of speech sound disorders
  • Early intervention-informed language development strategies
  • Functional communication training for everyday routines
  • AAC implementation and caregiver training
  • Individualized, family-centered therapy planning
