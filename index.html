Our Speech Pathology Services

Articulation Therapy

Help with clear pronunciation and sound production for children and adults.

  • Sound production training
  • Speech clarity improvement
  • Accent modification

Language Development

Support for understanding and expressing language effectively.

  • Vocabulary building
  • Grammar and syntax
  • Reading comprehension

Fluency Disorders

Treatment for stuttering and other fluency challenges.

  • Stuttering therapy
  • Fluency shaping techniques
  • Confidence building

Voice Therapy

Treatment for voice disorders and vocal health maintenance.

  • Voice rehabilitation
  • Vocal hygiene
  • Professional voice coaching

Swallowing Therapy

Assessment and treatment of swallowing difficulties (dysphagia).

  • Swallowing assessment
  • Safety strategies
  • Exercise programs

Cognitive Communication

Support for communication challenges related to brain injury or neurological conditions.

  • Memory strategies
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Social communication

Meet Your Speech-Language Pathologist

Wendy Arlint, M.S., CCC-SLP

Speech-Language Pathologist

Wendy brings extensive experience in helping individuals of all ages overcome communication challenges. She is dedicated to providing evidence-based treatments in a supportive, personalized environment.

Whether you're dealing with speech delays, language disorders, voice problems, or swallowing difficulties, Wendy is here to guide you on your journey to better communication.

Professional Credentials

  • Master of Science in Speech-Language Pathology
  • ASHA Certified (Certificate of Clinical Competence in SLP)
  • Licensed Speech-Language Pathologist
  • Ongoing continuing education in latest treatment methods

Areas of Specialization
